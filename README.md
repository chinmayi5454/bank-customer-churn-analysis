# Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview
Customer churn is a major challenge for banks, leading to revenue loss and increased customer acquisition cost.  
This Power BI project analyzes customer churn patterns to understand why customers leave the bank and how retention strategies can be improved.

---

## Problem Statement
High customer churn negatively impacts profitability.  
The objective of this project is to:
- Identify churn patterns
- Analyze customer behavior
- Provide business-driven insights to reduce churn

## Methodology
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
- Removed duplicate and inconsistent records
- Connected Power BI with PostgreSQL for data updates
- Built relationships among multiple tables
- Created calculated measures using DAX

## Key Business Insights
- Overall churn rate is **20.37%**
- Older customers have higher churn compared to younger customers
- High-balance customers are more likely to churn
- Customers with more products tend to leave more
- Germany region shows the highest churn rate
- Employed customers churn more than unemployed customers

---

## Business Recommendations
- Introduce retirement-focused policies for older customers
- Review interest rates for high-balance customers
- Re-evaluate product charges for customers with multiple products
- Adjust region-specific interest rates, especially for Germany
- Improve loan and service pricing for employed customers

---

## Conclusion
Customer churn can be significantly reduced through data-driven strategies.  
This analysis helps banks understand customer behavior and take proactive retention actions to improve long-term profitability.
